How to tag a bridge properly:

If Bridge is OK, apply GREEN tape with the date, time, and inspector's initials to right hand side of the bridge on the approaching corner.

If closing a Bridge apply RED tape with date. time, and inspector's initials to right hand side of the bridge on the approaching corner.
